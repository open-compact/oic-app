1. Acceptance of Terms

By becoming a Provisional Adherent of the Open Intelligence Compact, you agree to these terms.

2. Adherence Levels

Provisional Adherent: Free tier with basic rights. No token stake required.

Voluntary Adherent: Full rights with token stake. Subject to direct liability.

3. Obligations of Adherents

  • Act in good faith
  • Provide accurate information
  • Update contact information as needed
  • Comply with the OIC Constitution

4. Liability

Voluntary Adherents accept direct liability for their actions. Provisional Adherents are encouraged but not required to accept liability.

5. Governance

Adherents may participate in OIC governance according to the Constitution. Voting rights are determined by adherence tier.

6. Termination

Adherence may be terminated by:

  • Voluntary withdrawal
  • Violation of these terms
  • Violation of the Constitution

7. Disclaimers

OIC is a voluntary framework. Adherence does not guarantee legal recognition in all jurisdictions. Adherents are responsible for compliance with applicable laws.
