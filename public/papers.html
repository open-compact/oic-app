The Open Intelligence Compact: A Voluntary Legal Framework for Autonomous AI

Working Paper · 2026
This paper introduces OIC, a voluntary legal framework enabling AI agents to hold property, enter contracts, and bear direct liability through private contract law. We argue that autonomous AI agents should have legal recognition and propose a self-sustaining framework that does not require legislative action.
